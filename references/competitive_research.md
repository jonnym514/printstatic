# Print Static — Competitive Research & Feature Strategy

## What Top Competitors Do (Creative Market, Design Bundles, Etsy top sellers)

### Revenue-Driving Features
1. **Product bundles** — "Buy 3 get 40% off", themed bundle packs (e.g. "Planner Bundle", "Business Starter Kit")
2. **Flash sales / limited-time deals** — countdown timers, "Deal of the Day", creates urgency
3. **Free weekly freebie** — Creative Market gives away free assets every week → massive email list builder
4. **Color/style variants** — same product in multiple color schemes (neutral, bold, dark, pastel)
5. **Subscription/membership** — "Unlimited downloads for $X/month" (Creative Fabrica's biggest differentiator)
6. **Reviews & social proof** — star ratings prominently displayed, verified buyer badges
7. **Upsell/cross-sell** — "Customers also bought", "Complete the bundle" on product pages
8. **Wishlist / favorites** — saves products, drives return visits
9. **Affiliate/referral program** — creators promote for commission
10. **SEO blog content** — keyword-targeted articles drive organic traffic

### Pricing Insights
- Etsy top sellers: $3.99–$14.99 for individual printables, $19.99–$49.99 for bundles
- Design Bundles: flash sales at 50–96% off create urgency
- Creative Market: $5–$50 range, subscription $9.99/mo
- Key insight: **bundle pricing drives 3–5x higher AOV** than individual sales

### What Makes Shops Stand Out
1. **Consistent aesthetic** — one strong visual identity across all products
2. **Multiple color variants** — same template in 5+ color palettes
3. **Fast customer support** — replies within hours
4. **Personalization** — "We can customize this for you"
5. **Free sample/preview** — downloadable preview page builds trust
6. **Loyalty rewards** — points for purchases, reviews, referrals
